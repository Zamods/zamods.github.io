ApeValet provides a platform to post or schedule paid parking in various emirates, including Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Khorfakkan, and Al Ain. Users must follow the in-app instructions to choose the emirate, parking zone, and specify the duration of parking.

3. Notifications:

The App will send timely reminders, including a 15-minute warning before your parking expires and a 5-minute notice before a scheduled payment when using the scheduling feature.
