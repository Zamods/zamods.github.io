ApeValet App - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17th December 2023

Welcome to ApeValet, the all-in-one app for managing paid parking in the UAE. This privacy policy outlines our commitment to your privacy and explains that ApeValet does not collect any user data.

1. Information Collection:

ApeValet does not collect any personal or identifiable information from its users. We do not request access to your device's contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other personal data.

2. Usage Data:

We do not collect or track any usage data, including app usage patterns, preferences, or settings. ApeValet operates on a data-free model, ensuring your privacy is upheld.

3. Communications:

ApeValet does not use your device's communication features to send or receive any information. The app operates independently without the need for external communication.

4. Third-Party Services:

As there is no data collected by ApeValet, users do not have specific controls related to data privacy within the app. Your usage of ApeValet is entirely private and secure. However, please be aware that we use Google AdMob for displaying ads in the app. Google AdMob may collect and use certain device information for personalized advertising. You have the option to control personalized ads and limit ad tracking on your device based on your preferences. Adjust these settings in your device's privacy settings. Your decision on ad personalization is entirely at your discretion.

5. User Control:

As there is no data collected, users do not have specific controls related to data privacy within the app. Your usage of ApeValet is entirely private and secure.

6. Security:

While no data is collected, we prioritize the security of ApeValet. We implement standard security measures to protect the app from unauthorized access, ensuring a safe and private user experience.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy:

Any updates to this privacy policy will be communicated through the app's update release notes. Users are encouraged to review the release notes when updating the app.

8. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at apevalet@altmails.com.

By using ApeValet, you acknowledge and agree that no personal data is collected or processed. If you do not agree with this privacy policy, please refrain from using the app.

Thank you for trusting ApeValet with your parking needs!
